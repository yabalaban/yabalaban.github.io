Building with constraints

Building with constraints

Constraints are often seen as obstacles. But I've found them to be catalysts—forcing creativity where abundance might have bred complacency.

The 10KB challenge

Recently, I challenged myself to build a complete landing page under 10KB. No frameworks, no dependencies. Just HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.

The result wasn't just smaller—it was better. Every byte mattered. Every choice was intentional.

Why constraints work

  1. Focus: Limited options force clear priorities
  2. Creativity: Boundaries inspire novel solutions
  3. Performance: Less code means faster experiences

When you can't do everything, you learn to do the right things exceptionally well.

Embrace the limitation

The next time you face a constraint—whether it's time, budget, or technical—don't fight it. Lean into it. Let it guide you toward simplicity.

That's where the magic happens.
